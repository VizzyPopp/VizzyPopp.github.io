History

Founded by Christopher Doan, had its humble beginnings selling frozen treats from kiosk found in various malls around Houston. After traveling in Asia, Christopher Doan visited unique themed cafes. Due to the pandemic, he introduced themed experiences, transforming Popfancy into the popular pop culture cafe it is today.

Key Attractions

Visually stunning decor in different themes ranging from superheroes to video games. Note the themed menu items and how often they change. They often host fan meetups, cosplay contest and even screenings.
